About Us

Startup Internship program is a part of Entrepreneurship Cell FCRIT. Through SIP, we aim to collaborate with the start-ups willing to offer internship opportunities to the students of FCRIT. This initiative has been extremely beneficial for both start-ups as well as the students. Startups get a bunch of highly enthusiastic students, with a high susceptibility to learning new things; and creative minds that are determined on proving their mettle in the next big league of their life. SIP Internship season would kick off in the month of January. E-Cell through SIP help students get real life entrepreneurial experience. Startups from all over the world register with SIP and organize skype interviews or personal interviews with students for summer internships. Resume workshops, preparation sessions among more are conducted every year as part of the recruitment process with these startups.

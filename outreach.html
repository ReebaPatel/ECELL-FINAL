OUTREACH

The Outreach Program was a highly impactful event aimed at reaching out to students and imparting essential knowledge on entrepreneurship. Through engaging sessions, students learned about the fundamental qualities of successful entrepreneurs and explored various topics related to starting and sustaining a business. The program provided a unique opportunity for students to gain valuable insights, develop entrepreneurial skills, and connect with experienced mentors. The Outreach Program successfully inspired and empowered the next generation of entrepreneurs, leaving a lasting legacy of knowledge and motivation.

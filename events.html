OUR EVENTS

E-SUMMIT

The FCRIT Ecell E-Summit was a remarkable gathering of visionary entrepreneurs, industry experts, and inspiring thought leaders. The event featured insightful talks, engaging workshops, and valuable networking opportunities, igniting entrepreneurial journeys and making a lasting impact on all participants.

IDEATHON

The Ideathon was an exhilarating event where participants presented and pitched their groundbreaking startup ideas. Featuring a dynamic mix of aspiring entrepreneurs, industry mentors, and seasoned investors, the event fostered innovative thinking, invaluable connections, and collaborations.

OUTREACH PROGRAM

The Outreach Program was a highly impactful event that reached out to students, providing essential knowledge on entrepreneurship. Through engaging sessions, students learned about the qualities of successful entrepreneurs and various related topics, empowering the next generation of innovators.

AGNETHON

Agnethon was an exciting hackathon organized by Father Agnel's College Ecell, bringing together talented programmers, designers, and innovators. Participants collaborated intensively to solve real-world problems, showcasing their skills and creativity in a competitive environment.
